Our Story

A Vision Born from Love & Purpose

Precious English Medium School was founded by Mr. Raymond and Mrs. Nora Onouha — a couple driven by a deep conviction that every child in Qacha's Nek deserves access to world-class, English-medium education.

Recognising a critical gap in quality early-childhood and primary schooling in this mountainous region of Lesotho, the Onouhas channelled their professional experience and personal passion into building an institution that places the whole child at its centre.

The name "Precious" is no accident — it reflects the founders' belief that each learner is a treasure, worthy of devoted care, thoughtful teaching, and an environment where their unique gifts can flourish.

After careful planning and preparation, the school is proudly reopening for the 2026 academic year, ready to welcome its first cohort of young learners and begin what the Onouhas hope will be a legacy of excellence in Qacha's Nek and across Lesotho.

Our Programs

From Curiosity to Confidence

Two carefully crafted programs that meet learners exactly where they are and grow with them every step of the way.

🌱

Pre-School

Ages 3 – 5 · Play-Based Learning

Our Pre-School nurtures the natural wonder in every young child. Through rich sensory experiences, imaginative play, and gentle structure, we lay the social, emotional, and cognitive foundations that set learners up for life.

  • ✦
    Sensory Engagement – hands-on exploration that sparks discovery through touch, sight, sound, and movement.
  • ✦
    Play-Based Learning – structured play that builds language, creativity, and cooperative skills naturally.
  • ✦
    English Immersion – early, joyful exposure to English as the language of learning and communication.
  • ✦
    Social-Emotional Growth – teaching empathy, sharing, and self-regulation from the very start.

🏫

Primary School

Grades 1 – 7 · Academic Excellence

Our Primary School builds on the Pre-School foundation with a rigorous yet nurturing curriculum. We equip learners with strong academic skills while shaping the character and leadership qualities they'll carry for life.

  • ✦
    Literacy & Communication – deep reading, confident writing, and articulate spoken English across all subjects.
  • ✦
    Mathematics & Reasoning – logical thinking, problem-solving, and numeracy built step by step with strong conceptual grounding.
  • ✦
    Leadership & Character – values education that develops integrity, service, and responsible citizenship from an early age.
  • ✦
    Sciences & Creative Arts – broad exposure that sparks curiosity and allows every learner to discover where they shine.
